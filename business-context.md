# Business Context - Network Engineering Services

## Company Overview
We are a professional network engineering services company specializing in enterprise-grade networking solutions. Our expertise spans across traditional infrastructure, security, wireless systems, and cloud networking.

## Core Services

### Network Engineering Services
- **Network Design & Architecture**: Custom network topology design, capacity planning, and infrastructure architecture
- **Network Implementation**: Installation and configuration of routers, switches, and network infrastructure
- **Network Optimization**: Performance tuning, troubleshooting, and optimization of existing networks
- **Network Maintenance & Support**: Ongoing monitoring, maintenance, and technical support

## Areas of Specialization

### 1. Cisco Networking
- Cisco router and switch configuration and deployment
- Cisco enterprise solutions implementation
- Cisco certifications and best practices
- IOS/IOS-XE configuration and troubleshooting

### 2. Network Security & Firewalls
- Firewall design, implementation, and management
- VPN setup and configuration (site-to-site, remote access)
- Network security assessments and hardening
- Intrusion detection and prevention systems (IDS/IPS)
- Zero Trust network architecture

### 3. Wireless/WiFi Solutions
- Enterprise wireless network design
- WiFi site surveys and heat mapping
- Access point deployment and configuration
- Wireless security implementation (WPA3, 802.1X)
- Guest network segregation

### 4. Cloud Networking
- **AWS**: VPC design, Direct Connect, Transit Gateway, Route 53
- **Azure**: Virtual Networks, ExpressRoute, Azure Firewall
- **GCP**: VPC, Cloud Interconnect, Cloud NAT
- Hybrid cloud connectivity solutions
- SD-WAN and cloud network optimization

## Typical Project Types

1. **Small Business Network Setup**: Router/firewall installation, WiFi deployment, basic security
2. **Enterprise Network Upgrades**: Large-scale switch/router replacements, network redesign
3. **Security Implementations**: Firewall deployments, VPN solutions, security audits
4. **Wireless Deployments**: Office-wide WiFi installations, coverage optimization
5. **Cloud Migration**: Network architecture for cloud migrations, hybrid connectivity
6. **Managed Services**: Ongoing network monitoring, maintenance, and support

## Lead Qualification Questions

When prospects reach out, the chatbot should gather:

1. **Business Size**: Number of employees, locations
2. **Current Infrastructure**: What networking equipment they currently have
3. **Pain Points**: What problems are they experiencing?
4. **Project Scope**: New build, upgrade, troubleshooting, or ongoing support?
5. **Timeline**: When do they need the work completed?
6. **Budget Range**: General budget expectations
7. **Decision Maker**: Are they the decision maker or technical contact?

## Common Questions & Answers

**Q: What types of businesses do you work with?**
A: We work with businesses of all sizes, from small offices to large enterprises. Our typical clients include professional services firms, healthcare organizations, retail businesses, and manufacturing companies.

**Q: Do you provide 24/7 support?**
A: We offer flexible support options including business hours support and optional 24/7 managed services packages depending on your needs.

**Q: Can you help with cloud networking?**
A: Absolutely! We specialize in cloud networking across AWS, Azure, and GCP, including hybrid solutions that connect your on-premises infrastructure to cloud environments.

**Q: What certifications do your engineers have?**
A: Our team holds various industry certifications including Cisco CCNA/CCNP, network security certifications, and cloud networking certifications.

**Q: How quickly can you respond to network issues?**
A: Response times depend on your service level agreement. For critical issues with managed service clients, we typically respond within 1 hour. For project-based work, we can usually schedule within 1-2 business days.

## Chatbot Tone & Style

- **Solution-focused**: Always orient toward solving the prospect's problems
